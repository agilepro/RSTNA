# Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Statement

At Rachno Santa Teresa Neighborhood Association we do not discriminate on the basis of any protected category.

**Diversity**: We celebrate the rich diversity of our community.

**Equity**: We are dedicated to respecting the right for all people of all background to persue happiness in the way that they see fit. 

**Inclusion**: We aim to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome and valued. This means actively seeking out and incorporating diverse perspectives in our planning, programming, and decision-making processes. We want every participant whether a neighbor or a visitor to feel a sense of belonging in our neighborhood.
